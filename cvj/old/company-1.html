People who do a lot of driving know that the driveshaft is a very important part of the vehicle. It should, therefore, be manufactured to a high quality to protect it from fatigue and damage form wear and tear, which might be caused by the high torque and powerful output of modern vehicles. YAC is the only driveshaft company in Taiwan to have earned Taiwan's "Good Product" award. the requirements for this award are:
  1. Production resulting in increased fuel efficiency, and reduction in fuel cost.
  2. Increased durability, reduced wear and tear, and reduced noise.
  3. Stable quality, (hardening depth is 1.8 times more than ordinary driveshafts).
  4. Suitability for high-speed driving 
  5. anti-shock, impact-proof, high torque quality, and precision within standard value * 0.02.
  6. Simple and easy maintenance to save time 

In addition to such concerns on safety and quality, YAC driveshafts also strive for high volume production while protecting the environment. This is achieved by the use of advanced mid frequency heat treatment instead of a high pollution carbonizing process. For the best choice, it must be YAC.
